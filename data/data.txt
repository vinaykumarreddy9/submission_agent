Design, develop, and maintain Java Spring Boot-based backend microservices.

Refactor and migrate monolithic systems to cloud-native microservices.

Build and integrate RESTful APIs for internal and external services.

Work with AWS services (EC2, Lambda, S3, ECS, RDS) for deployment and scalability.

Implement CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes.

Participate in system design reviews, code reviews, and performance optimization.

Support enterprise-scale applications across finance, insurance, and healthcare domains.

Strong understanding of scalable, distributed system design

Preferred Background

Prior exposure to regulated domains (Banking, Insurance, Healthcare)

MS in Computer Science or equivalent from a reputed university
